2 weeks to discover Patagonia

Going to Patagonia before the start of my exchange in Buenos Aires was an obvious choice for me. So I consulted a guidebook to organize my trip and here's my trip summary

Some suggests of visits

The Argentine side of Patagonia

We visited El Calafate, where we went to the Perito Moreno glacier and Lago Roca. My favorite part was El Chalten, where we did a lot of hiking, including Lomo Del Pliegue Tumbado and Laguna Torre.

Here's a photo of my favorite moment in Patagonia

The Chilean side of Patagonia

We then crossed the border to Puerto Natales. We spent two days in the Torres Del Payne Park, which is more touristy than Argentina, but very pretty.
